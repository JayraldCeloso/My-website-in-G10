It takes a (mostly) reformed theif to catch a spy. Which is why Gilly Cobbler, Enchantasia's most notorious pickpocket, volunteers to stay locked up at Fairy Tale Reform School... indefinitely.

Alva (the wicked fairy who cursed Sleeping Beauty) has declared war on the princesses, and she wants the students of Fairy Tale reformed School to join her. As her criminal classmates give in to temptation, Gilly goes undercover as a royal Lady-in-waiting (don't laugh) to unmask a spy... before the mole can hand alva the keys to the kingdom.
